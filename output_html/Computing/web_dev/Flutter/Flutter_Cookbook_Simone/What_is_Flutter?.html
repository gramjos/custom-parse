Channels
  • Flutter segments it development streams
  • Each channel represents different level of stability of the flutter framework
  • As new features are being developed they are first released to the master channel and as the new features stabilize they great promoted to the beta channel and then finally to the stable channel
$ flutter channel
Flutter channels:
  master (latest development branch, for contributors)
  main (latest development branch, follows master channel)
  beta (updated monthly, recommended for experienced users)
* stable (updated quarterly, for new users and for production app releases)
